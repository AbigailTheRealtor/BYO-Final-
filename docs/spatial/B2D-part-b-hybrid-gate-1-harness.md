# B2D Part B — Hybrid Gate 1 Harness

**Phase 2 · Batch 2D Part B · Spatial Intelligence Platform**
Branch: `phase-2-batch-2d-part-b-hybrid-gate-1-harness`
Status: **cluster-free authoring complete. No PostGIS, no `SPATIAL_*` secrets, no corpus import, no Overture download, no infrastructure.**

This batch replaces the compliance-blocked, Google-derived Gate 1 approach with a
**license-clean, deterministic, CI-safe** harness, and de-Googles the ranking golden
master. It is the redefinition of Part B approved by the product owner (Option D +
Option E + golden-master remediation).

## Why Part B was redefined

Gate 1 (SSOT §9.3) is the acceptance gate for Phase 2. Its original design compared the
corpus's nearest-3 against a **frozen Google-labelled ground truth (844 rated rows)**.
That ground truth — and the ranking golden master derived from the same
`property_location_pois` rows — is Google Place **content committed indefinitely**, the
exact exposure SSOT §9.4 orders purged (Google caps caching at 30 days). The corpus side
also cannot run until a Class-2 cluster exists. So the buildable-now, compliance-correct
move is to replace the ground-truth strategy, not the corpus query.

## Scope (all Class-1)

| Option | Deliverable |
|---|---|
| **D — Synthetic benchmark** | `tests/Fixtures/Spatial/Gate1/synthetic-gate1-scenarios.json` — synthetic names/types/ratings/reviews + per-candidate `legitimate` labels. Zero Google-derived content. |
| **E — Runtime validation framework** | `Gate1RankSanityEvaluator` ranks scenarios through the real `LocationDnaRankingEngine`, counts embarrassments in the top-N window, and reports a rate vs the SSOT §9.3 ≤3% threshold. Surfaced via `spatial:gate1-validate`. |
| **Golden-master remediation** | `ldna:remediate-golden-master` strips business names, ratings, and review counts from `ranking-golden-master.json`; regenerates every `expected` block and re-baselines the digest. |

## Owner decisions honored

1. **Types unchanged.** The golden master's `types` (generic taxonomy tokens) are
   preserved; taxonomy behavior and ranking profiles are untouched.
2. **Ratings/reviews removed** from the golden master (not replaced). Rating-based scoring
   coverage moves to Option D's synthetic scenarios, which carry varied synthetic ratings
   (high / mid / low / **absent**) so `review_confidence` and `consumer_relevance` stay
   exercised.
3. **Exclusion rules / real corpus deferred to Class-2.** The evaluator measures the
   **pure ranking engine's** category discrimination over labelled synthetic scenarios. It
   does not touch `CATEGORY_EXCLUSION_RULES`, does not modify `LocationDnaPoiDistanceService`,
   and introduces no corpus dependency.

## The evaluator (Option E)

Provider-agnostic by construction: it consumes **raw candidate arrays** — the engine's
input shape, identical to `PoiBaselineDiffHarness` — so the same evaluator runs unchanged at
Class-2 against a real `CorpusPoiAdapter`. Ground truth swaps from synthetic labels to the
frozen corpus baseline; the pipeline does not move.

It reads only **rank order and POI identity**, never `ranking_score` (erratum E-48). The
`Gate1Report` structure carries no score-bearing key.

## Golden-master remediation

Deterministic and idempotent. Synthetic names are assigned by position
(`Synthetic POI 0001…`); ratings/review-count keys are dropped; **real coordinates and
listing ids are replaced with deterministic synthetic values** (contract below); and
`expected` + the digest are regenerated by re-running the engine over the synthetic inputs.
Re-running the command on an already-remediated fixture is byte-identical (verified).

- **Removed:** 995 business names, 844 ratings, 995 review counts, **1,098 real coordinate
  points** (995 candidate + 103 source), **real listing ids**. (No `place_id`/`vicinity`/
  address were present.)
- **Kept:** `types` (generic taxonomy/scoring tokens carried from the prior fixture — no
  names/coords/ratings/reviews/addresses/place-ids), `listing_type` (generic role), `category`;
  103 groups / 995 rows / 20 categories / 6 listings.
- **Digest re-baselined:** `cfc0c05c…` (Phase 1) → `496d4812…` (names/ratings/reviews) →
  **`498b683c…`** (final, after coordinate + listing-id synthesis) — in `_meta.golden_hash_sha256`
  and `PoiBaselineDiffHarnessTest::FROZEN_DIGEST`.

**Synthetic coordinate contract (v1).** Coordinates are a pure function of array position, so
no original value survives and none is a rounded/shifted/hashed/offset copy: every group's
source is the fixed point `(30.0, -90.0)`, and the candidate at 0-based index `i` sits at
`(round(30.0 + (i+1)*0.01, 6), -90.0)` — deterministic, well-separated distances that exercise
the engine without preserving the original spatial geometry. Original relative distances are
**not** preserved, by design. Listing ids are synthetic ordinals `1..6` by first appearance.

The golden master's job is unchanged: prove `LocationDnaRankingEngine` is deterministic
under refactor. De-Googling does not weaken that (E-48: the digest asserts engine
determinism, not historical Google reproduction).

## Scope limits (stated, not silent)

The synthetic benchmark validates the **harness pipeline** and the engine's **type
discrimination on well-separated cases**. It does **not** certify corpus quality and does
**not** exercise exclusion rules or adversarial distance-dominated tail cases — a very-close
parking lot still out-ranks far transit stops under the pure engine, which is precisely the
tail a real corpus needs exclusion rules for. That, and corpus-backed embarrassment
evaluation, are **Class-2**.
