Audit and improve the AI Questions section across all Create Offer Listing flows.

Scope:
Create Offer Listings only:
- Seller
- Buyer
- Landlord
- Tenant

Property type structure:
- Seller: Residential, Income, Commercial, Business, Vacant Land
- Buyer: Residential, Income, Commercial, Business, Vacant Land
- Landlord: Residential, Commercial
- Tenant: Residential, Commercial

Do not modify Hire Agent flows in this task.
Do not modify routes, database schema, migrations, controllers, or unrelated UI.
Do not remove required structured listing fields.
Only update AI Questions wording, duplicates, property-type relevance, categories, and placeholder text.

Goal:
The AI Questions section should become a practical FAQ / AI Knowledge Base based on questions real buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants, and agents commonly ask.

Important rule:
Do NOT repeat questions already captured elsewhere in the structured listing tabs.

Remove or rewrite AI questions that duplicate structured fields such as:
- price
- rent
- deposit
- beds/baths
- square footage
- property type
- city/county/state
- financing type
- lease term
- move-in date
- occupancy
- pet policy
- HOA fees
- zoning, if already captured
- compensation or broker terms

Keep or add questions that are common real-world questions users ask agents but are usually not captured elsewhere, such as:
- age of roof
- age of HVAC/AC
- age of water heater
- recent renovations
- average utility costs
- insurance/flooding history
- maintenance expectations
- neighborhood/lifestyle insights
- parking details beyond structured fields
- flexibility, motivation, concerns, and hidden selling points

Placeholder standard:
Update AI Question placeholders to this format:

Enter [question topic] (e.g., Example)

Examples:
- Enter roof details (e.g., Replaced in 2021)
- Enter HVAC details (e.g., New AC installed in 2023)
- Enter utility cost details (e.g., Average electric bill is $180/month)
- Enter seller flexibility details (e.g., Seller prefers a 30-day closing but may consider faster terms)

Avoid generic placeholders like:
- Optional — enter your answer here
- Describe...
- Type response...
- Enter answer
- N/A

Question organization:
Where possible, group AI Questions into clear categories such as:
- Property Condition & Maintenance
- Lifestyle & Location Insights
- Financial & Utility Insights
- Flexibility & Negotiation
- Hidden Selling Points
- Approval / Application Details
- Commercial Use & Operations
- Investment / Income Details
- Land Development Details
- Business Operations Details
