* a bad conditional that hides the enhancements section when parcel map is selected
* a watcher (`updated…`) that resets the enhancements array when parcel map is checked

### Required Behavior (Must Match)

* Checking parcel map must **not** hide, collapse, reset, or remove digital enhancements.
* Digital enhancements options must remain visible and selectable at all times (unless the user unchecks “Provide digital enhancements…” itself, if you’re using a nested sub-options UI).

✅ **Safeguard:** Do NOT store digital enhancement sub-options inside the same array as the top-level media services. Mixing them often causes accidental resets when one top-level option toggles.

✅ **Safeguard:** Parcel map is independent. It must never affect enhancements visibility.
