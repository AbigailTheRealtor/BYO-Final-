TENANT CRITERIA LISTING — LANDLORD FAQ QUESTIONS

Category: Tenant Readiness & Motivation
1. Is the Tenant actively looking to lease now?
2. How soon does the Tenant want to move?
3. Why is the Tenant moving?
4. Is the Tenant relocating, downsizing, upsizing, changing jobs, starting school, or moving for another reason?
5. Has the Tenant already viewed rental properties?
6. Has the Tenant submitted applications or lease offers elsewhere?
7. Is the Tenant under any move-in deadline?
8. Is the Tenant flexible on location, property type, rent amount, or lease terms?

Category: Income, Employment & Financial Strength
9. What is the Tenant’s approximate monthly income?
10. What is the Tenant’s employment status?
11. How long has the Tenant been with their current employer or income source?
12. Can the Tenant provide proof of income?
13. Can the Tenant provide bank statements or financial documentation if requested?
14. Does the Tenant meet common income requirements, such as 2.5x or 3x monthly rent?
15. Does the Tenant have savings available for move-in funds?
16. Is the Tenant able to pay first month’s rent, security deposit, and other move-in costs?

Category: Credit, Background & Rental History
17. What is the Tenant’s general credit profile?
18. Is the Tenant willing to complete a credit check?
19. Is the Tenant willing to complete a background check?
20. Does the Tenant have prior rental history?
21. Can the Tenant provide landlord references?
22. Has the Tenant ever been evicted?
23. Does the Tenant have any prior lease violations?
24. Is the Tenant willing to explain any credit, background, or rental history issues?

Category: Desired Property Criteria
25. What type of rental property is the Tenant looking for?
26. What locations, cities, counties, or neighborhoods is the Tenant considering?
27. What bedroom and bathroom count does the Tenant prefer?
28. What square footage range does the Tenant prefer?
29. Does the Tenant prefer furnished or unfurnished?
30. What property condition is acceptable?
31. What features are must-haves?
32. What features are deal-breakers?
33. Is the Tenant interested in HOA, condo, apartment, single-family, duplex, or townhome rentals?

Category: Lease Terms & Move-In
34. What monthly rent range is the Tenant comfortable with?
35. What is the maximum monthly rent the Tenant is willing to pay?
36. What lease length does the Tenant prefer?
37. Is the Tenant open to longer lease terms?
38. Is the Tenant open to shorter lease terms?
39. What move-in date does the Tenant prefer?
40. Is the Tenant flexible on move-in date?
41. Does the Tenant need to split move-in funds into payments?
42. Is the Tenant requesting any rent concession, move-in special, or discount?
43. Is the Tenant requesting renewal options?

Category: Occupancy, Pets & Vehicles
44. How many occupants will live in the property?
45. Who will occupy the property?
46. Does the Tenant have pets?
47. What type, breed, weight, and number of pets does the Tenant have?
48. Is the Tenant willing to pay pet deposit, pet fee, or monthly pet rent?
49. Does the Tenant have vehicles?
50. How many parking spaces does the Tenant need?
51. Does the Tenant need garage, covered, assigned, guest, or oversized parking?

Category: Utilities, Maintenance & Responsibilities
52. What utilities does the Tenant prefer to have included?
53. Is the Tenant willing to pay utilities separately?
54. Is the Tenant willing to handle lawn care?
55. Is the Tenant willing to handle pool care?
56. Is the Tenant willing to handle pest control?
57. Does the Tenant have any maintenance or accessibility needs?
58. Is the Tenant willing to follow HOA, condo, community, or property rules?

Category: Showing & Application Readiness
59. Is the Tenant available for showings?
60. How much notice does the Tenant need for showings?
61. Is the Tenant available for virtual showings?
62. Is the Tenant ready to apply quickly if interested?
63. Can the Tenant provide application documents quickly?
64. Who will be involved in the leasing decision?

Category: Landlord High-Intent Questions
65. Why should a Landlord consider this Tenant?
66. What makes the Tenant a strong applicant?
67. Is the Tenant serious and ready to move forward?
68. Can the Tenant submit an application or lease offer quickly?
69. What is the best way for a Landlord to respond to the Tenant?
70. What does the Tenant need from a Landlord to move forward?
