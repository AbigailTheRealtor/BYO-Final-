## Formatting Rules (MANDATORY)

### A) Percentage (%) Values

For **Buyer, Tenant, Landlord, and Seller** flows:

If the stored value represents a percentage (e.g., `5`, `5%`, `2.75%`):

* Normalize to a clean percent
* Append **exactly**: ` of Total Purchase Price`

**Valid outputs:**

* `5% of Total Purchase Price`
* `2.75% of Total Purchase Price`

---

### B) Flat Dollar Amounts ($)

If the stored value is a flat amount:

* Format as currency only (platform standard)

**Valid output:**

* `$5,000.00` (or `$5,000` if that is your standard)

❌ Do **NOT** append “of Total Purchase Price” to flat amounts.

---

### C) Empty / Null

* If no value exists, **do not render the line**.
