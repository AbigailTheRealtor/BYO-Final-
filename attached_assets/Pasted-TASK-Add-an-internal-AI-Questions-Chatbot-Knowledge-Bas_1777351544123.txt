BUYER CRITERIA LISTING — SELLER FAQ QUESTIONS

Category: Buyer Readiness & Motivation
1. Is the Buyer actively looking to purchase now?
2. How soon does the Buyer want to buy?
3. Why is the Buyer looking to purchase?
4. Is the Buyer relocating, upsizing, downsizing, investing, or purchasing for another reason?
5. Has the Buyer already viewed properties?
6. Has the Buyer submitted offers on other properties?
7. Is the Buyer under any time pressure or deadline?
8. Is the Buyer flexible on location, property type, or terms?

Category: Budget, Price & Offer Strength
9. What is the Buyer’s maximum budget?
10. Is the Buyer willing to offer above asking price for the right property?
11. Is the Buyer flexible on price?
12. How much earnest money deposit is the Buyer willing to offer?
13. Is the Buyer willing to increase the deposit for a stronger offer?
14. Is the Buyer requesting Seller credits, concessions, or closing cost assistance?
15. What price range is the Buyer most comfortable with?
16. Does the Buyer have funds available for closing costs and reserves?

Category: Financing / Offered Currency
17. Is the Buyer pre-approved?
18. Can the Buyer provide a pre-approval letter or proof of funds?
19. What financing or payment type does the Buyer plan to use?
20. Is the Buyer using cash, conventional, FHA, VA, seller financing, assumable financing, cryptocurrency, exchange/trade, lease option, lease purchase, NFT, or another structure?
21. Has the Buyer’s lender reviewed the Buyer’s documents?
22. How much is the Buyer putting down?
23. Does the Buyer have any financing conditions the Seller should know about?
24. Is the Buyer willing to switch loan programs or lenders if needed?
25. How quickly can the Buyer obtain loan approval?
26. Are there any appraisal concerns or financing limits?

Category: Desired Property Criteria
27. What property type is the Buyer looking for?
28. What locations, cities, counties, or neighborhoods is the Buyer considering?
29. What bedroom and bathroom count does the Buyer prefer?
30. What square footage range does the Buyer prefer?
31. What lot size, acreage, or outdoor space does the Buyer prefer?
32. What property condition is acceptable?
33. Is the Buyer open to repairs, renovations, or as-is properties?
34. What features are must-haves?
35. What features are deal-breakers?
36. Is the Buyer interested in furnished or unfurnished properties?
37. Is the Buyer interested in HOA, condo, or association communities?

Category: Offer Terms & Contingencies
38. What closing timeline does the Buyer prefer?
39. Is the Buyer flexible on the closing date?
40. What inspection period does the Buyer prefer?
41. Is the Buyer willing to shorten or waive inspection contingencies?
42. Does the Buyer require an appraisal contingency?
43. Does the Buyer require a financing contingency?
44. Does the Buyer need to sell another property first?
45. Is the Buyer requesting any personal property, appliances, furniture, or fixtures?
46. Is the Buyer requesting a home warranty?
47. Is the Buyer open to Seller leaseback or post-closing occupancy?
48. Are there any special terms the Buyer needs?

Category: Showing & Access
49. Is the Buyer available for showings?
50. How much notice does the Buyer need for showings?
51. Is the Buyer available for virtual showings?
52. Can the Buyer make quick decisions after viewing a property?
53. Who will be involved in the decision-making process?

Category: Closing Readiness
54. How quickly can the Buyer close?
55. Does the Buyer already have a title company, attorney, or closing preference?
56. Is the Buyer ready to sign documents electronically?
57. Does the Buyer need to be physically present for closing?
58. Are there any timing issues that could delay closing?

Category: Seller High-Intent Questions
59. Why should a Seller consider this Buyer?
60. What makes the Buyer’s offer strong?
61. Is the Buyer serious and ready to move forward?
62. Can the Buyer submit an offer quickly?
63. What is the best way for a Seller to respond to the Buyer?
64. What does the Buyer need from a Seller to move forward?
