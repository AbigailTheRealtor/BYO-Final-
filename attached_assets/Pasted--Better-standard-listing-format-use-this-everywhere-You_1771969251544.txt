### Better “standard” listing format (use this everywhere)

Your **Buyer** view (bubble/chip style) is the better standard for anything that can be **multi-select**. It’s faster to scan, looks modern, and matches the rest of your UI.

**Rule of thumb**

* **Chips (bubbles):** multi-select + lists (Cities, Counties, Property Styles, Conditions, Views, Amenities, Services, etc.)
* **Text line:** single-value fields (State, Min Beds, Heated SqFt, “Age-Restricted: Yes/No”, etc.)
* **Yes/No with details:** show as `Yes (detail)` (Pets: Yes (3), Pool: Yes (Private, Community))

✅ So yes — make **Tenant** match **Buyer** bubble style for those fields for consistency.

