# 1. Hide Public Default Compensation Terms

## Problem

The public Direct Hire profile pages currently display:

`Agent's Default Broker Compensation & Agency Agreement Terms`

This exposes detailed negotiation-specific compensation terms publicly before a user even begins the Direct Hire process.

These terms should not be publicly visible.

---

# Required Fix

On public Direct Hire pages:

* `/hire/{token}/buyer/{propertyType}`
* `/hire/{token}/seller/{propertyType}`
* `/hire/{token}/tenant/{propertyType}`
* `/hire/{token}/landlord/{propertyType}`

hide the entire public section titled:

## `Agent's Default Broker Compensation & Agency Agreement Terms`

including:

* commission structures
* flat fees
* percentages
* retainer fees
* early termination fees
* protection periods
* lease/purchase fee structures
* agency agreement terms
* referral fees

from public visitors.

---

# Public Replacement Section

Replace the hidden section with:

## Section Title

`Compensation & Agreement Terms`

## Public Message

`Detailed compensation and agreement terms are available during the Direct Hire request and negotiation process.`

Optional secondary line:
`Sign in or begin the hire process to review full terms.`

---

# Logged-In Visibility Rules

Show full compensation details ONLY when:

* user is logged in
  AND
* user is actively participating in the Direct Hire process

Examples:

* Request Changes / Counter Terms
* Accept & Submit Hire Request
* signed-in client reviewing negotiation terms
* signed-in agent viewing referral terms

Do NOT affect:

* marketplace negotiation visibility
* submitted hire request summaries
* internal negotiation flows

---
