----------------------------------------
UNIVERSAL OFFER COUNTER STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------

Use the same simple structure for Buyer, Seller, Landlord, and Tenant:

1. Client Info
2. Location / Target
3. Deal Terms
4. Qualification Signals
5. Additional Terms or Notes

Keep the form short and fast.

----------------------------------------
TENANT OFFER COUNTER FIELDS
----------------------------------------

Client Info:
- Full Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address

Location / Target:
- Areas of Interest
  Placeholder:
  Enter preferred cities, neighborhoods, counties, or ZIP codes

Deal Terms:
- Maximum Monthly Lease Price
  Placeholder:
  Enter maximum monthly lease price (e.g., $2,500)

- Desired Lease Length
  Dropdown options:
  Select
  Month-to-Month
  3 Months
  6 Months
  12 Months
  18+ Months
  Flexible / Unsure

- Move-In Date
  Date picker

Qualification Signals:
- Number of Occupants
  Placeholder:
  Enter total number of occupants

- Household Monthly Income
  Placeholder:
  Enter total monthly household income (e.g., $6,500)

Do NOT show Tenant Property Address fields.

ADDITIONAL TERMS OR NOTES
----------------------------------------

Add this field to ALL Offer Counter forms as the LAST field.

Label:
Additional Terms or Notes

Input:
Textarea

Placeholder:
Enter any additional terms, requests, or clarifications (e.g., flexible move-in date, including utilities, furnishing requests, preferred closing timeline, etc.)

Rules:
- Optional
- Store in existing meta/additional_details structure if available
- Do NOT create a new DB column unless no safe existing storage exists
- Do NOT parse into structured deal logic
- Do NOT let it override core deal fields
- Treat as supplemental context only

Display label on review/summary:
Additional Notes (Non-Binding Context)

Show in:
- Counter review screen
- Accepted Offer Summary, only if safe and already has a place for supplemental counter details
