Replit Instructions — Add Match Score to Competing Agent “View Bid” (Limited View) + Display Cards (Bidding Period)
PROBLEM (Current)

In Bidding Period listings:

Match Score is visible only when viewing your own full bid

Competing agent cards may show broker/services summary but no Match Score

Competing agent “View Bid” (limited competing view) shows:

Offered Services

Broker Compensation Terms
…but does not show Match Score

GOAL (Required)

For Bidding Period / Timed listings, once an agent has submitted a valid bid:

Match Score must display in ALL of these locations:

A) Agent’s own bid display card (no click required)
B) Competing agents’ bid display cards (no click required)
C) Agent’s own View Bid full view (already exists)
D) Competing agents’ View Bid limited view (the anonymized preview that shows Offered Services + Broker Comp Terms)

SAFEGUARDS (MUST FOLLOW)
DO NOT:

rename/delete any DB columns, field keys, migrations, or models

change bid submission, timer, accept/counter/reject logic

expose competitor identity (name, email, brokerage, license, phone, profile)

show timestamps or “last bidder”

show counter terms to other agents

show scoring formulas/weights or itemized per-field/per-service match lists beyond counts

show any data outside the allowed competing-bid scope:

Broker Terms (summary/terms only)

Offered Services (summary)

Match Score (module)

MUST:

enforce submit-to-unlock for competing bids

reuse the same Match Score component everywhere (single source of truth)

use snapshot-based score data to avoid drift

REQUIRED MATCH SCORE DISPLAY FORMAT (Must Match Existing Module)

Wherever shown, Match Score must display:

Overall Match badge: overall_percent%

Broker Terms: broker_terms_percent% + (X/Y fields matched)

Services: services_percent% + (X/Y services matched)

Footer label: Compared to Your Bid

Replace any “Compared to Your Original Bid” with Compared to Your Bid.

SECTION 1 — Competing Bid Display Cards (Required)

On anonymized competing bid cards (Agent 1 / Agent 2 / etc.):

Must show on card (no click)

Match Score module (full format above)

Offered Services summary (count or grouped summary)

Broker Compensation / Broker Terms summary (allowed fields only)

“View Bid” link (opens limited view)

✅ Match Score must be visible directly on the card without clicking View Bid.

SECTION 2 — Competing Agent “View Bid” Limited View (Required)

When agent clicks View Bid on a competing agent card:

Must display (in this limited competing-bid view)

Match Score module (full format above) — display near the top

Offered Services (same as current limited view)

Broker Compensation Terms / Broker Terms (same as current limited view)

This view remains anonymized and must not include identity or any other private data.

SECTION 3 — Own Bid Card + Own View Bid (Required)
Own bid display card must show Match Score (no click)

Even for the agent’s own card (on listing page / bidding period screen), Match Score must render on the card.

Own “View Bid” full view keeps Match Score

No change except ensure:

same format

same footer label (“Compared to Your Bid”)

SECTION 4 — Data / Payload Requirements (Critical)

All four views (own card, competing cards, own view, competing view) must have access to:

overall_percent

broker_terms_percent

broker_terms_matched_count

broker_terms_total_count

services_percent

services_matched_count

services_total_count

compared_to_label = "Compared to Your Bid"

Baseline Rule

“Compared to Your Bid” means:

other bid’s latest submitted version
vs

viewing agent’s latest submitted version
Draft edits don’t count until re-submitted.

SECTION 5 — QA Acceptance Criteria (Simple Tests)

After I submit on a bidding period listing, my bid card shows Match Score without clicking

After I submit, competing agent cards show Match Score without clicking

Clicking competing “View Bid” shows:

Match Score module

Offered Services

Broker Compensation Terms

No competitor identity is shown anywhere

No timestamps or “last bidder” messaging

Traditional listings remain private and unchanged

KEY LINE TO PREVENT MISIMPLEMENTATION (Put at top of dev task)

Match Score must render on BOTH the competing bid display card AND the competing “View Bid” limited preview, not just inside the user’s own bid view.
