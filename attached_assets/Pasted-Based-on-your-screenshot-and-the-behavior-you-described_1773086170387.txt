Based on your screenshot and the behavior you described, this is a **classic Select2 + Livewire edit-state sync problem**.

What’s happening:

* When you **click Edit**, the database values exist.
* Select2 **shows the item greyed out** (because it is technically selected in the DOM).
* But **Livewire doesn’t actually receive the value**, so:

This usually means the **Select2 change event is not syncing back to Livewire on edit load**.
