Replit Instructions — Fix “Acceptable Cities → Acceptable Counties” Auto-Mapping (Wrong County Showing)
Applies to: Hire a Tenant + Hire a Buyer listing creation (ONLY)
Issue

When a user selects Acceptable Cities (e.g., “Kenneth City, FL” or “Tierra Verde, FL”), the form auto-populates Acceptable Counties, but it is selecting the wrong county (e.g., “Alachua County, FL”).

Expected Behavior

When a city is selected, the system must populate the correct county for that city in that state.

If a city could resolve to multiple counties OR the system can’t reliably resolve the county, it must NOT guess. It must:

leave the county blank and/or

require the user to manually select/confirm county.
