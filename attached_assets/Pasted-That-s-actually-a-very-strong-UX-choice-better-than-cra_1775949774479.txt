# 🧠 Why this is better than 1-row

### 1. Clear hierarchy

* Top row = **make a decision**
* Bottom row = **review details**

### 2. Less clutter

* 4 buttons in one row can feel cramped
* especially with icons + text

### 3. More intuitive

Users think:

> “Do I accept / counter / reject?”
> THEN
> “Let me view the terms”

---

# ⚠️ Important Design Rules

### Row 1 (decision buttons)

* same size
* evenly spaced
* strong colors:

  * Accept = green
  * Counter Back = yellow
  * Reject = red

---

### Row 2 (secondary buttons)

* smaller visual weight
* neutral/outlined style
* aligned left (not centered)
