#664 — Nationwide City & Municipality Alias Enrichment

What & Why:
The ZIP/city normalization system now works, but some real-world city names, municipality variants, and common user-entered names are still missing. Users often type “St. Petersburg,” “St Petersburg,” “St. Pete Beach,” “Saint Pete Beach,” “Pass-a-Grille,” etc. The system needs to recognize official names, USPS names, municipality names, local names, and common abbreviations.

Goal:
Add nationwide city/municipality alias support so autocomplete and ZIP/city lookup work for common real-world search terms, especially “St.” / “Saint” / “St Pete” variations.

STRICT SAFEGUARDS:
- DATA-ONLY unless a small alias lookup utility/table is required.
- DO NOT modify Livewire components.
- DO NOT modify Blade files.
- DO NOT modify Select2/autocomplete JavaScript.
- DO NOT overwrite existing city/county/state rows.
- DO NOT delete existing ZIP rows.
- DO NOT touch users, listings, bids, drafts, or accepted bid summaries.
- DO NOT run migrate:fresh.
- Preserve existing IDs.
- Dry-run/report first.
