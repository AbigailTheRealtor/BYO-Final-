* **Listing Creator Acknowledgement**
* **Agent Acknowledgement**

* **Agent Acknowledgement**

## My recommendation

Unless the architecture clearly treats the non-agent side as the listing owner in every flow, use:

* **Listing Creator Acknowledgement**
* **Agent Acknowledgement**

Why:

* clearer than “Authorized User”
* role-neutral across Buyer, Seller, Landlord, Tenant
* less awkward than hardcoding Tenant/Buyer/Seller/Landlord in a shared block
* fits platform logic well

### Important

First audit whether the acknowledgement data model and sign flow assume a role-specific label anywhere else.
