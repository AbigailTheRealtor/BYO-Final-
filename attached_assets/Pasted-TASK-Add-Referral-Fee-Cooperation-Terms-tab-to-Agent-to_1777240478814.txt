TASK: Add Referral Fee & Cooperation Terms tab to Agent-to-Agent Counter Terms only

GOAL:
When an Agent is countering another Agent’s bid, add a separate Counter Terms tab immediately after:
“Broker Compensation & Agency Agreement Terms”

New tab name:
“Referral Fee & Cooperation Terms”

This tab should contain the Agent-to-Agent Referral Fee question and should auto-fill from the Agent’s submitted bid, while remaining editable during the counter process.

CRITICAL SAFEGUARDS:
- Agent-to-Agent counterbids ONLY.
- Do NOT change Seller, Buyer, Tenant, or Landlord consumer/client listing creation flows.
- Do NOT change public listing views.
- Do NOT rename or remove existing fields.
- Do NOT change database schema unless the existing referral_fee_percent field cannot support this.
- Do NOT alter Broker Compensation, Agency Agreement, Offered Services, or Additional Details logic.
- Preserve all existing counterbid behavior, validation, state handling, and Accepted Bid Summary generation.

SCOPE:
Only update the Agent-to-Agent counter terms UI/form flow where an Agent counters another Agent’s bid.

Find the counter terms component/view used for Agent-to-Agent bidding.

Search for:
- “Counter Terms”
- “Broker Compensation & Agency Agreement Terms”
- “Offered Services”
- “referral_fee_percent”
- “Private Compensation & Agreement Terms”
- agent-to-agent conditions already in use

Report the exact files before editing.

“Broker Compensation & Agency Agreement Terms”

“Referral Fee & Cooperation Terms”
