## IMPORTANT DEFINITIONS

A. FIXED BASELINE CATALOG TOTAL
This means:

* the count of the standard service options/categories before dynamic selected sub-options and before custom Additional Services entries are added

B. DYNAMIC SELECTED BASELINE TOTAL
This means:

* the actual live denominator used for Match Score on a specific listing
* based on what the user actually selected
* including dynamic sub-options and custom Additional Services entries when selected

## CRITICAL RULE FOR PHOTOGRAPHY, VIDEO & VIRTUAL MEDIA

For both LANDLORD and SELLER listings:

Photography, Video & Virtual Media has:

* a fixed base count of 7
* PLUS dynamic selected sub-options under:
  "Provide digital photo enhancements"

Each selected enhancement sub-option counts as +1.

So:

Photography, Video & Virtual Media baseline formula =
7 + number of selected digital photo enhancement sub-options

If all 6 enhancement sub-options are selected:
7 + 6 = 13

This applies to Seller and Landlord where that service structure exists.

---

## CRITICAL RULE FOR ADDITIONAL SERVICES

“Other – Specify additional services as needed”
is dynamic.

Each custom Additional Service entered counts as +1.

Examples:

* 1 custom service entered = +1
* 2 custom services entered = +2
* 3 custom services entered = +3

## COMMERCIAL LANDLORD CLARIFICATION

Commercial Landlord:
