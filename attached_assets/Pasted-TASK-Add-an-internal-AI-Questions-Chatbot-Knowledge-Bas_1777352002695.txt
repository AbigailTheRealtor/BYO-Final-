LANDLORD PROPERTY LISTING — TENANT FAQ QUESTIONS

Category: Pricing, Costs & Fees
1. What is the monthly rent, and what does it include?
2. What is the security deposit?
3. Are there any move-in fees?
4. What is the application fee?
5. Are there admin, processing, lease preparation, or other fees?
6. Is the security deposit refundable?
7. Are there any non-refundable fees?
8. Are utilities included in the rent?
9. Which utilities are included?
10. What is the average utility cost, if known?
11. Is renter’s insurance required?
12. Are there pet fees, pet deposits, or pet rent?
13. Are there parking fees?
14. Are there late payment fees?
15. Is there a discount, incentive, move-in special, or promotion?

Category: Application Process & Requirements
16. How does a Tenant apply?
17. What documents are required to apply?
18. What are the income requirements?
19. Is there a minimum credit score?
20. Are co-signers or guarantors accepted?
21. How long does approval usually take?
22. Is the application fee refundable if denied?
23. Can a Tenant apply before seeing the property?
24. What background checks are performed?
25. Are Section 8, housing vouchers, or assistance programs accepted?
26. Are there any automatic denial criteria?
27. Are there any HOA, condo, or association approval requirements?

Category: Move-In & Lease Terms
28. When is the property available?
29. What is the minimum lease term?
30. Are short-term leases available?
31. What is required to move in?
32. How much is due at move-in?
33. Can move-in costs be split into payments?
34. What happens if the Tenant needs to break the lease?
35. Are renewal options available?
36. Is the lease start date flexible?
37. Are longer lease terms preferred or incentivized?

Category: Property Details
38. How many bedrooms and bathrooms does the property have?
39. What is the square footage?
40. Is the property furnished or unfurnished?
41. What appliances are included?
42. Is there a washer and dryer in the unit?
43. Is there air conditioning and heating?
44. Has the property been updated or renovated, and when?
45. What is the age and condition of major systems, including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roof, or water heater where applicable?
46. Is there outdoor space, balcony, patio, yard, storage, or common area access?

Category: Pets
47. Are pets allowed?
48. What types of pets are allowed?
49. Are there breed restrictions?
50. Is there a weight limit?
51. What are the pet fees, deposits, or monthly pet rent?
52. Are service animals or assistance animals handled according to applicable law?

Category: Parking & Transportation
53. Is parking included?
54. Is parking assigned?
55. Is guest parking available?
56. Is garage, covered, or secured parking available?
57. Are there vehicle restrictions?
58. Is public transportation nearby?

Category: Maintenance & Management
59. Who handles maintenance?
60. How does a Tenant submit maintenance requests?
61. How quickly are repairs usually handled?
62. Is there emergency maintenance?
63. Who handles lawn care, pool care, pest control, or exterior maintenance?
64. Are Tenants allowed to make minor repairs or modifications?

Category: Location & Neighborhood
65. What area or neighborhood is the property in?
66. What’s nearby, including shopping, restaurants, parks, beaches, schools, or highways?
67. How far is it from work centers, major roads, public transit, or local attractions?
68. Are there any community, building, or neighborhood details Tenants should know?

Category: Policies & Rules
69. Is smoking allowed?
70. Is subleasing allowed?
71. Are there HOA, condo, building, or community rules?
72. Are guests allowed long-term?
73. Are there noise, parking, pet, occupancy, business-use, or exterior-use rules?
74. Are there restrictions on decorating, painting, mounting TVs, or making changes?

Category: High-Intent Tenant Questions
75. Is the property still available?
76. Can a Tenant schedule a showing?
77. What does a Tenant need to do to secure the property?
78. How fast can a Tenant move in?
79. Are there any current specials or promotions?
80. What is the best next step for an interested Tenant?
