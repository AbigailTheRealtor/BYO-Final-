SELLER LISTING — BUYER FAQ QUESTIONS

Category: Pricing, Costs & Financing
1. What is the asking price, and is the Seller open to negotiation?
2. Are there any HOA, condo, or association fees? If yes, what are the amounts and what do they include?
3. What are the estimated property taxes?
4. What closing costs should a Buyer expect?
5. How much earnest money deposit does the Seller prefer?
6. Are there any lender-related fees, seller-paid fees, or transaction costs the Buyer should know about?
7. What are the estimated monthly ownership costs, if known?
8. Is the Seller willing to contribute toward Buyer closing costs or credits?
9. Are there any special assessments, pending assessments, or upcoming fee increases?
10. What is included in the sale price?

Category: Financing & Pre-Approval
11. Does the Seller require a Buyer to be pre-approved or provide proof of funds before making an offer?
12. What financing types will the Seller consider?
13. Are FHA, VA, conventional, cash, seller financing, assumable financing, cryptocurrency, exchange/trade, lease option, lease purchase, NFT, or other payment structures acceptable?
14. Is there a minimum down payment the Seller prefers or requires?
15. Are there any financing types the Seller will not consider?
16. How quickly does the Seller want loan approval or proof of funds confirmed?
17. Will the Seller consider a Buyer switching lenders during the transaction?
18. Are there any appraisal concerns or financing concerns Buyers should know about?

Category: Property Details
19. How many bedrooms and bathrooms does the property have?
20. What is the square footage?
21. What year was the property built?
22. Has the property been updated or renovated, and when were the updates completed?
23. What appliances, fixtures, or personal property are included?
24. What is the age and condition of the roof?
25. What is the age and condition of the HVAC system?
26. What is the age and condition of the water heater?
27. What is the age and condition of major systems, including plumbing and electrical?
28. Is the property in a flood zone?
29. Is the property furnished or unfurnished?
30. Are there any known defects, repairs needed, or condition issues?

Category: Offer & Negotiation
31. How should a Buyer submit an offer?
32. Are there currently multiple offers or strong interest?
33. How competitive is the current offer situation?
34. Is the Seller willing to negotiate price?
35. Is the Seller willing to negotiate terms other than price?
36. What contingencies will the Seller consider?
37. What inspection period does the Seller prefer?
38. How long does the Seller usually need to respond to an offer?
39. Will the Seller consider backup offers?
40. Are there any offer terms that would make an offer stronger?

Category: Inspections & Due Diligence
41. Can the Buyer have the property inspected?
42. What inspections are recommended or expected?
43. Are there any known inspection issues?
44. What happens if inspection issues are found?
45. Will the Seller consider repair requests or credits?
46. Is the property being sold as-is?
47. Is a Seller disclosure available?
48. Are there any permits, code issues, or open violations known?

Category: Closing & Possession
49. What closing timeline does the Seller prefer?
50. Is the Seller flexible on the closing date?
51. Who does the Seller prefer to use for title/closing, if anyone?
52. When can the Buyer take possession?
53. Does the Seller need post-closing occupancy or a leaseback?
54. Can closing be delayed if needed?
55. What documents will the Buyer need to sign or provide?

Category: Location & Neighborhood
56. What area or neighborhood is the property in?
57. What schools is the property zoned for, if known?
58. What nearby amenities, shopping, restaurants, parks, beaches, or highways should Buyers know about?
59. How far is the property from major roads, airports, employment centers, or local attractions?
60. Are there any neighborhood, community, HOA, or lifestyle details Buyers should know?

Category: High-Intent Buyer Questions
61. Is the property still available?
62. Can a Buyer schedule a showing?
63. How does a Buyer make an offer?
64. What does a Buyer need to do to secure the property?
65. How quickly can a Buyer close?
66. What is the best next step for an interested Buyer?

CHATBOT REQUIREMENTS:

- Allow aliases/search terms internally so chatbot can match user wording.

- Chatbot should check structured listing fields first, then these AI answers, then fallback.
- Do not hallucinate answers. If unanswered, chatbot should say the information was not provided and offer to connect the user or ask a follow-up.
