# Ask AI — Roadmap & Guardrails

This document defines the full Ask AI feature roadmap, build order, hard guardrails, allowed data sources, prohibited behaviors, future task sequence, and stop rule. It exists so all future Replit tasks for Ask AI stay phased, controlled, and non-destructive.

---

## 1. Current Status

- **Intelligence Stack Audit #1770** has passed.
- **Approved components** (in place or cleared for use): Property DNA, Location DNA, Buyer/Tenant Avatars, Compatibility Scores, Offer Analysis results, and Marketing Intelligence Engine outputs.
- **Phase 1 gating rule**: No Ask AI task may begin implementation until Phase 1 (structured data wiring and read-only context assembly) is confirmed complete and reviewed. Each subsequent phase is gated by completion of the phase before it.

---

## 2. Ask AI Build Order

### Phase 1 — Context Assembly (Read-Only Wiring)
Assemble structured data from approved sources into a read-only context object. No UI. No OpenAI calls. No scoring logic. Goal: confirm that Ask AI can see the right data before it is allowed to speak.

### Phase 2 — Prompt Engineering & Response Contracts
Define prompt templates, output schemas, and response contracts for each Ask AI use case. All prompts must be grounded exclusively in the context object assembled in Phase 1. No hallucination paths. No free-form generation without a structured anchor.

### Phase 3 — Backend Integration (OpenAI Wiring)
Wire the context object and approved prompts to OpenAI. Implement rate limiting, error handling, and response validation. No user-facing UI yet. Internal testing only.

### Phase 4 — Read-Only UI Surface
Introduce the first user-facing Ask AI entry point: a read-only explanation panel. Users may ask questions; Ask AI may only answer using data already visible on the page. No recommendations, no rankings, no qualifying conclusions.

### Phase 5 — Offer Analysis Explanation Layer
Allow Ask AI to explain Offer Analysis results in plain language. Scope is limited to explaining strengths, weaknesses, tradeoffs, scoring factors, and differences between offers. Ask AI may not choose a winning offer or recommend acceptance or rejection.

### Phase 6 — Compatibility & DNA Explanation Layer
Allow Ask AI to explain Compatibility Scores, Property DNA, Location DNA, Buyer/Tenant Avatars, and Marketing Intelligence outputs. Ask AI may explain what the scores mean and how they were derived. Ask AI may not recalculate, override, or independently generate any DNA or score.

### Phase 7 — Guided Q&A & Onboarding Assistance
Allow Ask AI to answer structured onboarding questions and guide users through platform workflows using approved documentation and structured data. No legal advice. No fair-housing conclusions. No promises about outcomes.

---

## 3. Hard Guardrails

- **Out-of-order prohibition**: No phase may be implemented before the previous phase is confirmed complete. Skipping phases is not permitted under any circumstances.
- **OpenAI restriction**: OpenAI (or any external LLM) may not be called until Phase 3. Any task that introduces an OpenAI call before Phase 3 is complete must be rejected and re-scoped.
- **UI restriction**: No Ask AI UI component may be created before Phase 4. Stub components, placeholder panels, or hidden elements that anticipate Ask AI are not permitted in earlier phases.
- **Scoring restriction**: Ask AI may not produce, recalculate, modify, or override any score, compatibility rating, DNA value, or offer analysis result at any phase.
- **Form restriction**: Ask AI may not pre-fill, suggest, or modify form field values. It is a read layer, not a write layer.
- **Migration restriction**: No database migration may be created as part of any Ask AI task unless it is explicitly scoped to store Ask AI conversation history (Phase 4 or later) and has been reviewed and approved in advance.

---

## 4. Ask AI Must Only Use Structured Data

Ask AI is permitted to read and interpret data exclusively from the following approved sources:

- **Property DNA** outputs (as generated by the Property DNA engine; Ask AI may not generate Property DNA independently)
- **Location DNA** outputs (as generated by the Location DNA engine)
- **Buyer/Tenant Avatars** (as generated by the Avatar engine)
- **Compatibility Scores** (as produced by the Compatibility Engine)
- **Marketing Intelligence Engine** outputs
- **Offer Analysis Engine** results
- **Listing fields** stored in the platform database (structured, user-entered data)
- **Bid fields** stored in the platform database (structured, user-entered data)
- **Accepted Bid Summaries** (as stored in `accepted_bid_summaries`)
- **Platform-defined configuration** (display config, field labels, role definitions)
- **Approved governance documents** — Ask AI may reference platform-approved markdown governance files to answer questions about its own capabilities, boundaries, and disclosure requirements. Approved documents include:
  - `ASK_AI_ROADMAP_AND_GUARDRAILS.md`
  - `ASK_AI_KNOWLEDGE_MAP.md`
  - `ASK_AI_DISCLOSURE_REQUIREMENTS.md`

No external data sources, web searches, real-time market feeds, or invented context are permitted.

---

## 5. Ask AI Must Not

- **Invent facts**: Ask AI must never assert a fact not present in the approved structured data sources. If data is absent, Ask AI must say so explicitly.
- **Rank offers or listings**: Ask AI must not produce a ranked list of offers or listings, or imply that one is superior to another in a way that goes beyond explaining scoring factors.
- **Qualify or disqualify parties**: Ask AI must not conclude that a buyer, tenant, seller, or agent qualifies or does not qualify for any transaction, loan, program, or outcome.
- **Render legal or fair-housing conclusions**: Ask AI must not provide legal advice, interpret fair-housing law, or draw any conclusion that could constitute a fair-housing determination or legal opinion.
- **Reference protected classes**: Ask AI must not reference, infer, or consider any protected class characteristic (race, color, religion, national origin, sex, disability, familial status, or any other protected class under applicable law) in any response.
- **Make predictions**: Ask AI must not predict future property values, market movements, likelihood of offer acceptance, or any other forward-looking outcome.

## 7. Stop Rule

If any Ask AI task — at any phase — produces output that:

- Calls OpenAI before Phase 3 is confirmed complete,
- Renders a user-facing UI before Phase 4 is confirmed complete,
- Modifies any score, DNA value, compatibility rating, or offer analysis result,
- Pre-fills or modifies any form field,
- References a protected class,
- Asserts a fact not present in approved structured data,
- Introduces a database migration not explicitly scoped and pre-approved,

**the task must be stopped immediately, all changes reverted, and the task re-scoped before resuming.**

The stop rule is absolute. It supersedes delivery pressure, timeline constraints, and user convenience arguments.

---

## 8. Ask AI Is An Interpretation Layer

Ask AI is an **interpretation layer**, not a decision-maker.

Its sole purpose is to interpret, summarize, and explain existing structured data that has already been computed by the Intelligence Stack. Ask AI does not make decisions. It does not replace human judgment. It does not act as a proxy for the platform's legal, financial, or transactional functions.

Users remain fully responsible for all decisions made on this platform. Ask AI explanations are informational only. They do not constitute professional advice of any kind (legal, financial, real estate, lending, or otherwise).

Ask AI must always be presented to users in a way that makes this interpretation-only role clear.

---

## 9. Property DNA Dependency Rule

- Ask AI **may consume** Property DNA, Location DNA, Buyer/Tenant Avatars, and Marketing Intelligence outputs produced by their respective engines.
- Ask AI **may explain** what Property DNA, Location DNA, Avatars, Marketing Intelligence outputs, Compatibility Scores, and Offer Analysis results mean in plain language.
- Ask AI **may not independently calculate** any DNA value, avatar classification, compatibility score, or marketing intelligence output.
- Ask AI **may not override** any value produced by the Property DNA engine, Location DNA engine, Avatar engine, Marketing Intelligence engine, or Compatibility Engine.
- If DNA or Avatar data is absent for a given record, Ask AI must state that the data is not yet available and must not attempt to infer or reconstruct it.

The Property DNA engine, Location DNA engine, and Avatar engine are the sole authoritative sources for their respective outputs. Ask AI is a downstream consumer only.

---

## 10. Offer Analysis Dependency Rule

- Ask AI **may explain** Offer Analysis results in plain language.
- Ask AI **may describe** the strengths, weaknesses, tradeoffs, scoring factors, and differences between offers as reflected in the Offer Analysis Engine's output.
- Ask AI **may not choose** a winning offer.
- Ask AI **may not recommend** that a user accept or reject any specific offer.
- Ask AI **may not imply** that any offer is objectively better or worse than another in a way that goes beyond explaining what the scoring factors reflect.
- Final acceptance and rejection decisions remain entirely with the platform users (sellers, landlords, agents, or their clients).

The Offer Analysis Engine is the sole authoritative source for offer scoring. Ask AI explains its outputs; it does not supplement, correct, or extend them.

---

## 11. Intelligence Stack Hierarchy

Ask AI sits at the top of the Intelligence Stack as a downstream consumer of all outputs below it. The hierarchy is fixed and must not be violated.

```
1. Property DNA Engine
        ↓
2. Location DNA Engine
        ↓
3. Buyer/Tenant DNA Engine
        ↓
4. Buyer/Tenant Avatar Engine
        ↓
5. Compatibility Engine
        ↓
6. Marketing Intelligence Engine
        ↓
7. Offer Analysis Engine
        ↓
8. Ask AI  ← consumes all of the above; produces none of them
```

**Rules of the hierarchy:**

- Each layer consumes outputs from the layers below it. No layer may consume outputs from a layer above it.
- Ask AI (layer 8) sits above the entire stack. It consumes outputs from all seven layers below it.
- Ask AI does not feed back into any layer below it. It is a terminal consumer.
- Ask AI does not become a source-of-truth for any data. It is an explanation layer only.
- The hierarchy may not be restructured, collapsed, or bypassed by any Ask AI task.

Any Ask AI implementation that causes data to flow upward in this hierarchy (i.e., an Ask AI output influencing a DNA value, avatar, score, or analysis result) violates this rule and must be stopped and reverted immediately.
